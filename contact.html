At Tutor U we strive on providing students in the Charlotte area with all the possibilities to excel academically. We offer our services to any student altough we do have an application process as we have to be selective to whom we can offer our services. We cover transportation and tuition costs for the tutoring centers.

First Steps

The application process is as follows: after filling our form below, our team will review the information provided. If eligible we will email send an email within 24 hours containing the application. Once we receive your application, Our team will review it and reach back out if interested.

Interview process

If your student is selected for one of our spots we will schedule an interview with their primary caretaker, and the student. Our team will inform you of our decision at the end of the interview.
